--[ PRESENTATIONS & ACTIVITIES ]

There will be one stage. Common stage. Named "Common sense stage". If you want
to participate, you must contribute. Add your topic to presentations list on
404.txt file [1] and open a pull request. Set topic title or workshop name as PR
title, add short description in the PR description field and wait. If we decide
your topic meets the spirit of 404, it will be approved. We are interested in
hacking, making, building, coding, brewing, hobbing, art, workshops. We are not
interested in travelling stories, life journeys, startups, commercial sh*t,
"intro into some framework" type of topics.

--[ SOUND ]

In the true spirit of the camp, there will be a variety of sounds, and we
already have some traditions. We'll start firmly on Thursday with sweat-inducing
ravey sounds while contemplating what we have right now; as you know, there is
no techno on a dead planet.

After the kick-off of the sessions on Friday, we'll open the stage with the
"synths corner" to cater to the most ornate listener with the endless stream of
dissonant bleeps and bloops. One thing, though - no succulents will be allowed
near the vicinity of synths.

Finally, on Saturday, we'll break the marathon of the talks sessions in the
evening to bring you more relaxing sounds for the last evening in the camp. Come
with a sleeping bag if you're not afraid to be absorbed in the sonic journey
emanating from the mist.

Timetable: TBA
